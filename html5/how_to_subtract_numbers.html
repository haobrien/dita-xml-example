How to subtract numbers

Here you will find instructions to use the subtration feature of the JPS Calculator Application.

Before you begin, you must understand the concept of subtraction.

  1. Enter the first number.
  2. Press the minus (-) key.
  3. Enter the second number.
  4. Press the equals (=) key.
The result of the subtraction is displayed in the calculator.

You want to subtract 2 from 3. The equation will look like: 3-2=1. You enter 3. Press minus. Enter 2. Press equals.

The result 1 appears on the screen.
